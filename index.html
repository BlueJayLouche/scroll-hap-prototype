Scroll-Scrub Video

Two approaches for buttery-smooth scroll-driven animation

🖼️
WebP Sequence
Simple & Reliable

Convert video to WebP frames, preload them, and display on scroll. The simplest approach that works everywhere.

  • No WASM complexity
  • Works in all browsers
  • Excellent WebP compression
  • Easy to implement
Try WebP Demo →
🎬
HAP + WASM
GPU-Accelerated

Use your Rust HAP decoder compiled to WASM. Decode on-demand and upload compressed DXT textures directly to GPU.

  • Extremely memory efficient
  • Handles 4K smoothly
  • Instant scrubbing
  • Silky GPU playback
